Backtest design

Rolling-origin protocol

1. SnapshotFreeze features exactly as they would have appeared on each scoring date.
2. ScoreRank all eligible CBSAs and counties with only available data.
3. ObserveWait three to six months for unemployment and payroll outcomes.
4. CompareTrack lift, precision, recall, calibration, and rank stability by geography type.

Label definitions

First observable outcomes

Unemployment spikeLocal unemployment rises at least 1.0 percentage point over six months.
Payroll declineEmployment falls year over year or turns negative for two consecutive releases.
Claims shockInitial claims index breaches local historical threshold.
